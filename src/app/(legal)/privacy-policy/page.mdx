# Privacy Policy for Qwen-Image-Layered

## Introduction
Welcome to **Qwen-Image-Layered**, a professional service that provides AI-powered image editing, enhancement, and transformation while keeping your data safe. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control your data.

## Information We Collect
We collect only what is needed to deliver and improve the service:

1. **Account Information**
   - Name, email, and authentication details you provide when signing up.

2. **Uploaded Images & Outputs**
   - Photos, screenshots, scans, and other assets you upload for editing and enhancement, plus the resulting processed images, thumbnails, and processing data.

3. **Usage Data**
   - Actions in the app (e.g., jobs created, status and timestamps), error logs, and performance metrics to keep the service reliable.

4. **Device & Technical Data**
   - Browser, device type, operating system, screen size, and IP-derived region to optimize performance and security.

5. **Cookies & Similar Technologies**
   - Session cookies and preference cookies to keep you signed in and remember settings. You can control cookies via your browser.

6. **Payment Information**
   - Billing details processed by our payment providers (e.g., card brand, last four digits, billing email). We do not store full card numbers.

## How We Use Your Information
- Provide, operate, and improve Qwen-Image-Layered, including editing accuracy, processing speed, and reliability.
- Authenticate users, prevent abuse, and secure the platform.
- Offer support, account notifications, and critical service updates.
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

## Data Storage & Retention

## Sharing & Disclosure

## Security
We use encryption in transit, access controls, and monitoring to protect your data. No online service is 100% secure, so please use strong passwords and contact us if you suspect unauthorized access.
